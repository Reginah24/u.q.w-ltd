About U.Q.W Ltd

U.Q.W Ltd (also known as U.Q.W Clearing Agency or U.Q.W Ltd Clearing and Forwarding) is a licensed customs clearing and forwarding agency based in Rwanda. We are officially recognized by the Rwanda Revenue Authority (RRA) as a licensed Customs Agent.

Operating at major customs points including Gikondo and Rubavu border posts, we specialize in cargo documentation, customs clearance, and freight forwarding services. Our commitment to efficiency and compliance ensures your goods move smoothly through customs.
