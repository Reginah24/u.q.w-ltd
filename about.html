About U.Q.W Ltd

Your Trusted Licensed Customs Clearing & Forwarding Partner

Our Story

U.Q.W Ltd, also known as U.Q.W Clearing Agency or U.Q.W Ltd Clearing and Forwarding, is a licensed customs clearing and forwarding agency based in Rwanda. We are officially recognized by the Rwanda Revenue Authority (RRA) as a licensed Customs Agent, demonstrating our commitment to professional and compliant cargo handling services.

Operating at key customs points including Gikondo and Rubavu border posts, we have built a reputation for efficiency, reliability, and compliance. Our expertise in cargo documentation, customs clearance, and freight forwarding ensures that your goods move smoothly through Rwanda's customs system with minimal delays.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, reliable, and compliant customs clearing and forwarding services that facilitate seamless trade and cargo movement in Rwanda, while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and customer service.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and efficient customs clearing and forwarding agency in Rwanda, recognized for our speed, accuracy, compliance, and commitment to facilitating international trade.
