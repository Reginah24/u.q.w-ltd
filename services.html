What We Offer

RRA licensed customs services tailored to your cargo needs

Customs Clearance

As an RRA licensed customs agent, we provide comprehensive customs clearance services for both imports and exports. Our expert team ensures your cargo is processed efficiently, minimizing delays and ensuring full compliance with Rwanda's customs regulations.

  • Import Clearance Processing
  • Export Documentation
  • Duty & Tax Calculation
  • Regulatory Compliance

Freight Forwarding

Our freight forwarding services ensure your cargo moves safely and efficiently from origin to destination. We coordinate all aspects of transportation, handling, and delivery.

  • Air Freight Coordination
  • Sea Freight Management
  • Road Transport Logistics
  • Cargo Tracking & Updates

Cargo Documentation

Proper documentation is critical for smooth customs clearance. We handle all required paperwork with precision and accuracy, ensuring your cargo meets all regulatory requirements.

  • Bill of Lading Processing
  • Commercial Invoice Preparation
  • Certificate of Origin
  • Packing List Verification

Border Operations

We operate at major customs points in Rwanda, providing on-ground support at border crossings to ensure fast processing of your cargo.

  • Gikondo Customs Operations
  • Rubavu Border Services
  • On-site Customs Representation
  • Real-time Status Updates

We issue Electronic Billing Machine (EBM) receipts for all transactions. Our client portal allows you to access your receipts anytime, anywhere.

  • 24/7 Online Access

  • Secure Client Portal

Consultation & Advisory
